Frequently Asked Questions

I’m not sure if therapy is for me. Should I book?

The first initial consultation is completely free! You are not committed to any further sessions after the first appointment. 

Having an initial session will give you a sense as to whether therapy is for you. Our clients often find that this first experience is helpful in deciding what they need and if we are a good fit together.

What happens in the initial session and why is it only 15 minutes?

This free 15-minute consultation is usually very informal and provides an opportunity for you and me to get to know each other. You’ll tell me a bit about why you are seeking counseling and your goals for therapy. I might follow with some questions designed to better understand your specific needs and to provide an idea of how the two of us would work together. Then, it will be open for you to ask any questions you might have about the therapy process, my therapy style, or to clarify any information on payments, availability, and schedules. 

Unfortunately, due to legal and ethical restrictions, I cannot offer you actual therapy during this consultation. However, it will give me the basics to jumpstart your client file and treatment plan if you chose to be come a client.

What is your cancellation policy?

48 hours. If you need to cancel for any reason less than 48 hours prior to your appointment, you will be charged a fee equal to the full session cost. If you have not paid for the session yet, this fee will be charged to the credit card on file 

How do I book an Emergency Therapy session? 

Although I encourage clients to work towards self-reliance between sessions and adhere to our usual therapy schedule, I do keep selected embargo slots open for those who need it.
